How do alerts trigger?

Alerts are triggered when the AI system detects a significant cultural moment based on real-time data from sources like PeakMetrics or TikTok trends. The system automatically notifies the relevant teams and phases for review and action.

Can clients access the platform?

Client-facing interfaces are available for review only after internal QA and approval. Most platform features are designed for internal team use, with select outputs and dashboards shared with clients as needed.

Where does audience data come from?

Audience data is sourced from platforms like StatSocial and other approved data providers. Synthetic personas are generated using this data where available, and are used to inform campaign strategy and creative development.

How are metrics and reporting handled?
