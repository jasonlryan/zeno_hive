Intelligence Hub: Detection & Alerts

Centralized trend and alert insights from TikTok, PeakMetrics, and other sources are surfaced here first to inform all downstream actions. The system provides real-time alerts for emerging cultural moments, trend summaries, and actionable signals. Metrics tracked include velocity, engagement, longevity, and audience alignment.

Alert Triggers & Reporting:

  • Trigger: Significant cultural moment detected by AI (e.g., via PeakMetrics, TikTok trends)
  • Routing: Automated notification to Ideation phase and relevant teams
  • Actions: Review, prioritize, and initiate creative response
  • Metrics: Alert delivery time, engagement, false positive rate, brand relevance

Process Definition

The Detection phase serves as the foundation of the AI Hive system, continuously scanning the digital landscape to identify emerging cultural moments that could be relevant for brand engagement. This always-on monitoring system combines multiple data sources and AI analysis to distinguish between fleeting trends and meaningful cultural shifts with staying power.

1

Continuous Monitoring

24/7 scanning across social platforms, news sources, podcasts, and cultural touchpoints using Peak Metrics API and custom monitoring tools.

2

Signal Analysis

AI-powered analysis of engagement patterns, velocity metrics, and cultural context to identify potential moments of interest.

3

Trend Classification

Categorization of detected signals based on longevity predictions, audience alignment, and brand relevance potential.

4

Alert Generation

Automated notifications to the Ideation phase when significant cultural moments are detected that match client parameters.
