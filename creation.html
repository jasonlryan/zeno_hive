Process Definition

The Creation phase transforms approved creative concepts into production-ready campaign assets using advanced AI generation tools. This automated production system creates channel-agnostic content that can be adapted across multiple platforms while maintaining brand consistency and creative quality through human oversight and approval processes.

1

Concept Translation

AI interpretation of approved creative concepts into detailed production briefs with visual and messaging specifications.

2

Multi-Format Generation

Automated creation of assets across multiple formats and platforms using text-to-image and text-to-video AI tools.

3

Brand Compliance Check

Automated verification against brand guidelines including visual identity, tone of voice, and messaging standards.

4

Human Review & Refinement

Creative team evaluation and approval with iterative refinement process for optimal asset quality and brand alignment.

AI Generation Tools & Technologies

Text-to-Image Generation

Advanced AI models for creating high-quality visual assets from creative concept descriptions with brand-specific style training and consistency controls.

Input: Creative briefs, brand guidelines
Process: AI image generation, style consistency
Output: Brand-aligned visual assets

Text-to-Video Creation

AI-powered video generation for creating dynamic content including motion graphics, animated sequences, and short-form video content for social platforms.

Input: Video concepts, storyboards
Process: Video generation, motion design
Output: Platform-ready video content

Automated Brand Compliance

AI systems trained on brand guidelines to ensure all generated assets maintain visual identity consistency, appropriate tone, and messaging alignment.

Input: Generated assets, brand standards
Process: Compliance verification, quality check
Output: Brand-compliant final assets

Multi-Platform Optimization

Automated adaptation of core creative assets to meet specific platform requirements including dimensions, file formats, and technical specifications.

Input: Master assets, platform requirements
Process: Format optimization, sizing adaptation
Output: Platform-specific asset variants
