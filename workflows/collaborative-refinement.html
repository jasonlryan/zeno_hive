Interactive process where human feedback directly improves AI output through real-time concept iteration and strategic direction adjustment

Ideation Phase
Human-in-Loop

Workflow Definition

The Collaborative Refinement workflow enables real-time iterative improvement of AI-generated concepts through structured human feedback. This process allows creative teams to guide AI output toward optimal solutions while maintaining creative control and ensuring alignment with strategic objectives and brand requirements.

1

Initial Concept Presentation

AI-generated concepts are presented to human reviewers with contextual information, relevance scoring, and initial strategic recommendations.

2

Human Feedback Capture

Structured collection of human insights including creative direction, strategic adjustments, and specific improvement suggestions.

3

Real-time AI Iteration

AI system immediately incorporates human feedback to generate refined concepts, maintaining creative momentum and collaboration flow.

4

Convergence & Optimization

Iterative refinement continues until concepts achieve optimal balance of creativity, strategic alignment, and execution feasibility.

Refinement Tools & Capabilities

Side-by-side comparison tools for evaluating multiple concept variations with scoring, annotation, and decision-making support.

Process: Comparative analysis, scoring
Output: Ranked concepts with rationale

Structured feedback capture system that translates human input into actionable AI refinement parameters for immediate concept improvement.

Input: Human feedback, concept status
Process: Feedback translation, parameter adjustment
Output: Refined AI parameters

Tools for modifying strategic parameters and creative direction based on evolving campaign objectives and market insights.

Output: Updated strategic parameters

Input: Iteration history, quality metrics

Output: Quality progression reports
