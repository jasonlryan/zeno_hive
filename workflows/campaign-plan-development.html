Ideation Phase

Workflow Definition

The Campaign Plan Development workflow transforms approved creative concepts into comprehensive campaign strategies. This process encompasses strategic planning, channel mapping, timeline development, resource allocation, and success metrics definition to ensure campaign concepts are executable and aligned with business objectives.

1

Concept-to-Strategy Translation

Approved creative concepts are analyzed and expanded into strategic frameworks that define campaign objectives, target audiences, and core messaging architecture.

2

Channel Strategy Development

Optimal channel mix determination based on audience behavior, content format requirements, and campaign objectives with platform-specific adaptations.

3

Timeline & Resource Planning

Development of detailed execution timelines, resource requirements, and milestone definitions to ensure campaign deliverability and success.

4

Success Metrics Framework

Definition of KPIs, measurement methodologies, and success criteria aligned with campaign objectives and business goals.
